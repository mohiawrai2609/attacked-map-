CRITICAL · CYB  United Kingdom · Automotive

Ransomware halts Jaguar Land Rover production across three UK plants

Jaguar Land Rover

A ransomware intrusion forced JLR to suspend assembly at Halewood, Solihull and Castle Bromwich. Initial vectors point to a compromised remote-access vendor account. Production loss is estimated at 1,000 vehicles per day while systems are rebuilt from clean backups.

What to do → If you operate OT-connected manufacturing, isolate vendor remote-access accounts and enforce MFA on all third-party tunnels today.

HIGH · DAT  United States · Healthcare

2.1M patient records exposed in misconfigured cloud bucket

MedCore Systems

A publicly readable storage bucket exposed names, dates of birth and insurance IDs for 2.1 million patients. The bucket was indexed by a third-party scanner before being secured. No ransomware involved — pure misconfiguration.

What to do → Run an external bucket-exposure scan across your own cloud estate; default-deny public access at the org policy level.
